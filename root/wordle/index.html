HOW TO PLAY

  • You must try to guess the word
  • You have 6 guesses
  • Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word
  • The color of each tile will change and give you information about that letter
  • Green means that letter is in the word, in that spot
  • Yellow means that letter is in the word, in a different spot
  • Grey means that letter is not in the word at all.
